Compliance & Regulation

You're in safe hands

We operate in a regulated industry and take that responsibility seriously. Here's how we stay compliant.

FAIS
Act

FAIS Act — Financial Advisory & Intermediary Services

Eazy F&I Solutions holds an authorised FSP licence under the FAIS Act, 37 of 2002. All financial advice and intermediary services are provided by qualified, fit-and-proper representatives.

NCA

National Credit Act

All credit applications are processed in compliance with the NCA, Act 34 of 2005, including responsible affordability assessments and full disclosure of all credit costs.

POPIA

POPIA — Data Privacy

Your personal information is processed lawfully and securely under POPIA, Act 4 of 2013. We only process what we need, retain it for the minimum necessary period, and never sell your data.

TCF

Treating Customers Fairly

We subscribe to the FSB's TCF outcomes framework. Your interests are considered in every recommendation we make — full stop.
