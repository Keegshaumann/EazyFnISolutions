Common Questions

Quick answers

How long does it take to get an approval?

Most applications receive an initial decision within 4–24 hours. Complex cases or those requiring additional documentation may take up to 48 hours. We'll always keep you updated.

Does applying affect my credit score?

Our initial assessment involves a soft enquiry which does not affect your score. A full credit check is only conducted once you have consented and are proceeding with a specific application.

Do you charge a fee for your services?

No. Our services to individuals are free. We earn a commission from the lending institution once a deal is concluded. This does not affect your interest rate or deal terms.

I have a poor credit record — can you still help?

Possibly. We have access to specialist lenders who consider the full picture, not just a credit score. Be upfront with us and we'll give you an honest assessment.

I'm self-employed — can I get vehicle finance?

Yes. Self-employed applicants need to provide 6 months' bank statements and supporting financial records. We know which lenders are most favourable to this profile.

How is my personal information protected?

All data is processed in compliance with POPIA (Act 4 of 2013). We only collect what we need, retain it for the minimum required period, and never sell or share your data without consent.
