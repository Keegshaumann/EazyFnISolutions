The Process

How the partnership works

Simple integration. Real results. Here's how we work alongside your F&I department from day one.

1

Register & Onboard

Complete our dealer registration. We set up your dedicated contact and agree on deal flow processes. Typically operational within 48 hours.

2

Submit Deals Your Way

Send us customer applications via your preferred channel — email, WhatsApp, or direct system integration. We adapt to your workflow.

3

We Work the Lenders

Your application hits multiple lenders simultaneously. We negotiate the approval and structure the deal for maximum approval rate and F&I revenue.

4

You Receive the Approval

We deliver a clean, structured approval with full documentation. You present to the customer. We handle any lender queries that follow.

5

Deal Pays Out

We manage the payout process and ensure your dealership's income is settled correctly and on time. No chasing. No delays.
