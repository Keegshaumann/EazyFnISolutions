Privacy Policy

How Eazy F&I Solutions collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Effective Date: 1 January 2025
Last Updated: 1 January 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to Eazy F&I Solutions, FSP Licence No. 28260, mandated under IDA Risk Management (Pty) Ltd, an authorised Financial Services Provider under the Financial Advisory and Intermediary Services Act, 37 of 2002 (FAIS Act). We are committed to processing your personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 4 of 2013 (POPIA).

1. Who We Are

Eazy F&I Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a vehicle finance intermediary operating in South Africa. We act as an intermediary between you and lending institutions — we do not lend money directly. Our physical address is 191 Gouws Avenue, Raslouw AH, Centurion. You can contact our Information Officer at admin@eazyfi.co.za.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal information:

  • Identity information: Full name, South African ID number or passport number, date of birth
  • Contact information: Email address, phone number, residential address
  • Financial information: Employment status, income, bank statements, credit history, existing financial obligations
  • Vehicle information: Details of the vehicle you wish to finance
  • Business information: Company registration details, director information, financial statements (for business applicants)
  • Communication data: Records of correspondence and enquiries submitted via our website or other channels
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and cookies (see Section 9)

3. How We Collect Your Information

We collect personal information directly from you when you:

  • Submit an enquiry or application via our website
  • Contact us by email, phone, or WhatsApp
  • Are referred to us by a dealer partner
  • Enter into a service agreement with us

We may also receive information from third parties such as credit bureaux and our dealer partner network, where permitted by law and with your knowledge.

4. Why We Process Your Information

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To assess your vehicle finance application and submit it to suitable lenders
  • To communicate with you about your application and our services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations under the FAIS Act, NCA, and POPIA
  • To conduct affordability assessments as required under the National Credit Act, 34 of 2005
  • To maintain records as required by law
  • To respond to general enquiries
  • To improve our services and website (using anonymised or aggregated data)

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information on the following lawful grounds under POPIA:

  • Consent: Where you have given us explicit consent, such as when submitting an enquiry or application
  • Contractual necessity: Where processing is necessary to provide the services you have requested
  • Legal obligation: Where we are required by law to process your information (e.g., under the FAIS Act or NCA)
  • Legitimate interest: Where processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests, provided these are not overridden by your rights

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Lending institutions and banks: For the purpose of submitting and processing your finance application, with your consent
  • Credit bureaux: As required under the NCA for affordability and credit assessments
  • IDA Risk Management (Pty) Ltd: As our mandating principal, where required for regulatory compliance
  • Regulatory authorities: The Financial Sector Conduct Authority (FSCA), the Information Regulator, or other competent authorities where legally required
  • Service providers: Third-party technology and administrative service providers who assist us in operating our business, subject to appropriate data processing agreements

All third parties with whom we share data are required to handle your information securely and in accordance with applicable law.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. Generally:

  • Application and transaction records are retained for a minimum of 5 years in accordance with the FAIS Act and NCA requirements
  • General enquiry records are retained for 12 months, unless a longer period is required
  • Records subject to legal hold are retained until the matter is resolved

Once no longer required, personal information is securely deleted or anonymised.

8. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to deletion: You may request deletion of your information, subject to our legal retention obligations
  • Right to object: You may object to the processing of your information where we rely on legitimate interest
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time (this will not affect processing carried out prior to withdrawal)
  • Right to lodge a complaint: You may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@eazyfi.co.za. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website (using anonymised data)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of information transmitted to our website.

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the Information Regulator as required by POPIA.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be published on our website with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

13. How to Contact Us & Complaints

For privacy-related queries or to exercise your rights, contact our Information Officer:

  • Email: admin@eazyfi.co.za
  • Address: 191 Gouws Avenue, Raslouw AH, Centurion

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa:

  • Website: www.inforegulator.org.za
  • Email: complaints.IR@justice.gov.za

You may also contact the Financial Sector Conduct Authority (FSCA) for complaints relating to financial advisory services:

  • Website: www.fsca.co.za
