Eligibility

What you need to apply

We keep the paperwork simple. Here's what we typically need to process your application quickly.

  • South African ID document or valid passport
  • Latest 3 months' payslips (salaried) or 6 months' bank statements (self-employed)
  • Proof of residence (not older than 3 months)
  • Latest 3 months' bank statements
  • Driver's licence (for the vehicle being financed)
  • Vehicle details (for a specific purchase) or budget range

NCA Compliance

All credit applications are processed in accordance with the National Credit Act (NCA), Act 34 of 2005. We conduct responsible affordability assessments to ensure you're not over-committed.

Your data is protected

We process all personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013. Your information is used only for the purpose of processing your credit application and is never sold to third parties.
